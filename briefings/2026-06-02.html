Update ASIS Security Management June 1, 2026

Privacy governance for video, access, and analytics: make security data defensible

ASIS highlights a growing imperative: treat physical security systems as data platforms that require governance, not just operation. Video, access control, biometrics, and analytics generate evidence that must be purpose-bound, access‑controlled, logged, and retained/deleted under auditable rules. The piece outlines concrete steps—role‑based access, formal request/authorization workflows, automated retention/deletion, and purpose-by-zone design—to reduce privacy debt and strengthen chain‑of‑custody. For healthcare and universities deploying cameras, body‑worn video, and AI analytics, this is a timely blueprint to satisfy policy, privacy, and discovery obligations while keeping investigations efficient and defensible.
